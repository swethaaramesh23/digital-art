Building the Future of Art

We believe in a world where creators are fairly compensated and art is globally accessible.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Stackly was born out of a simple observation: digital artists were creating incredible value, but capturing very little of it.

We built Stackly to be a premium, decentralized marketplace that leverages blockchain technology to ensure provenance, authenticity, and fair royalties for creators. Today, we are proud to host thousands of top-tier artists and millions of passionate collectors.

Our Mission

To empower digital creators with the tools they need to build their brand, protect their work, and earn a sustainable living from their passion.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for digital art ownership, fostering a community where art transcends physical boundaries and borders.

Our Core Values

Trust & Security

We prioritize the safety of our creators and collectors above all else through audited smart contracts.

Community First

Stackly is built by and for the community. We actively listen and evolve based on your feedback.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what is possible in the digital art and Web3 space.
