FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple — click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar and fill in your details. You can register as a Collector to browse and purchase artwork, or apply as an Artist to start showcasing and selling your creations. The entire process takes less than two minutes.

How do I list my artwork for sale?

Once your Artist application is approved, head to your Artist Dashboard and click "Upload Artwork." You can upload high-resolution images, set your price, add a title, description, and relevant tags. Your listing will go live on the marketplace immediately after you confirm all the details.

What are the platform fees?

Stackly charges a flat 2.5% service fee on every successful sale, which covers platform maintenance, payment processing, and artist support. There are no hidden listing fees or monthly subscription charges — you only pay when you make a sale. Artists keep 97.5% of every transaction.

How do payments work?

We support major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and selected cryptocurrency wallets for purchases. Artist earnings are accumulated in your Stackly wallet and can be withdrawn to your linked bank account or crypto wallet at any time. Payouts are typically processed within 3–5 business days.

Can I edit my listing after publishing?

Yes, you can update the title, description, tags, and price of your artwork at any time from your Artist Dashboard. However, the artwork image itself cannot be changed once published to maintain buyer trust and provenance integrity. If you need to replace the image, you can delist the current artwork and create a new listing.

How do I contact support?

You're already in the right place! Use the contact form above to send us a detailed message, or email us directly at hello@stackly.art. For urgent issues, you can also call us during business hours at +91 70106 92732. Our support team aims to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
