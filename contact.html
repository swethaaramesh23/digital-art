Frequently Asked Questions

What is Stackly?
Stackly is a premium decentralized marketplace where users can discover, collect, and sell digital art. We utilize blockchain technology to verify ownership and ensure creators receive royalties on secondary sales.
How do I set up a wallet?
You can set up a wallet using popular providers like MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or WalletConnect. Once installed, simply click the "Login" button and follow the prompts to connect it to our platform.
What fees does Stackly charge?
We charge a flat 2% fee on all primary and secondary sales to maintain the platform. Gas fees required by the blockchain are separate and paid directly to network miners.
How do I apply as an Artist?
Go to the Signup page and select "Artist" as your role. Our curation team reviews all applications to ensure high-quality standards across the marketplace. You will hear back within 48 hours.
