How It Works

Start your NFT journey in three simple steps.

1. Set up your wallet

Once you've set up your wallet of choice, connect it to Stackly by clicking the wallet icon in the top right corner.

2. Create your collection

Click My Collections and set up your collection. Add social links, a description, profile & banner images, and set a secondary sales fee.

3. List them for sale

Choose between auctions, fixed-price listings, and declining-price listings. You choose how you want to sell your art.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Stackly.

What is an NFT?

An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique digital asset verified using blockchain technology, ensuring authenticity and ownership.

How do I buy art on Stackly?

Connect your digital wallet (like MetaMask), fund it with Ethereum, browse our marketplace, and click "Buy Now" or place a bid on an artwork.

Are there any fees?

Stackly charges a standard 2.5% transaction fee on all secondary sales. Minting fees depend on current Ethereum gas prices.
